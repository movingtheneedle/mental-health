what can cause mental health issues?

There's a wide range of factors, including, but not limited to: traumatic experiences (example: childhood neglect/abuse, etc.), stressful situations (example: work/school-related, money issues), genetics/heredity, other conditions, relationships, complications during pregnancy, brain injury, bullying, social media, poverty/discrimination, and substance abuse. All of these can trigger reactions in the brain, affecting the mental health of the individual.
